Our Story

The VHotel stood tall and majestic for decades, it had been a beacon of hospitality, welcoming guests from all walks of life into its elegant embrace. The hotel's story began in the early 1999s when it was founded by a visionary hotelier named Revaldo. Revaldo had always dreamt of creating a place where people could escape the pressures of everyday life and find solace in luxury and comfort.
Revaldo spared no expense in crafting his dream hotel. He sourced the finest materials and enlisted the expertise of renowned architects and interior designers to bring his vision to life. The result was a stunning architectural masterpiece, adorned with intricate carvings, grand chandeliers, and exquisite artwork that told stories of distant lands.
